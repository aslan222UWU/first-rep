Welcome to Waffy & Coffee!

At Waffy & Coffee, we believe that life’s sweetest moments are best enjoyed with a perfect cup of coffee and a freshly made waffle.

Our Coffee

We take pride in every cup we serve. Our coffee beans are carefully selected from the finest growers around the world, ensuring that each sip is smooth, flavorful, and satisfying. Whether you prefer a robust espresso, a creamy cappuccino, or a sweet caramel latte ,our expert baristas are here to make your coffee just the way you like it.

The Waffles

What pairs better with coffee than a warm, crispy waffle? At Waffy & Coffee, we offer an array of waffle options, from classic bubble waffles to creative combinations topped with fresh fruits, ice cream, syrups, and more. Whether you’re in the mood for something sweet or savory, our waffles are made to perfection, with just the right amount of crunch on the outside and softness on the inside.
